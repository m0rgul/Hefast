  3DP-OPECR (Hefast) is a cloud-based application which helps 3D printer hubs in better organizing their production activities, estimating and forecasting materials consumption and in improving the communication with their clients.

  Due to the flexibility of the system, the hubs can be small (one person with a printer at home) or big (a prototyping or rapid manufacturing factory with several machines). We very conservatively estimate the total accessible market to be 20.000 3D printers (growing fast!).

  While there certainly is competition on the market, we took the best features and added new ones to create a more intuitive and easy to use application which innovates also by an additional layer of Big Data Analytics and Business Intelligence and the usage of FIWARE technologies.
